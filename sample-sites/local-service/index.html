FAQ

Common questions, straight answers.

If you don't see your question here, call us at (855) 555-0199 and we'll answer right away.

How quickly can you get to my home?

For emergencies, we aim to arrive within 2 hours. For non-urgent service calls, most appointments are available same-day or next-day. You'll receive a 30-minute heads-up text before your technician arrives.

Do you charge extra for evenings and weekends?

No. We believe honest pricing means the same rate regardless of when you call. There are no overtime fees, weekend surcharges, or holiday premiums. Ever.

What does "upfront pricing" actually mean?

Before any work begins, your technician will diagnose the problem and give you a written quote. You approve the price before we touch anything. No surprises on the invoice.

Are your technicians employees or subcontractors?

All BluePeak technicians are direct employees — never subcontractors. They're background-checked, drug-tested, uniformed, and drive marked BluePeak vehicles.

What's covered under your satisfaction guarantee?

If you're not satisfied with any work we perform, call us within 30 days and we'll return to make it right at no charge. If we can't fix the issue to your satisfaction, we'll refund the service cost in full.

Do you offer financing for larger repairs?

Yes. We offer 0% APR financing plans for qualifying projects over $500, including equipment replacements and major installations. Ask your technician for details, or mention it when you call.

The Artist

Ten years. One approach: your story, drawn in ink.

Kai Mercer has been tattooing professionally since 2014, building a practice rooted in custom design, careful placement, and a genuine obsession with the craft. Every piece is drawn from scratch — no flash, no repeats.

Specializing in fine line, blackwork, geometric, and neo-traditional styles, Kai works across a wide range of aesthetics while maintaining a commitment to clean linework and lasting results.

Based out of Axiom's private studio in East LA, bookings are taken by consultation only. Walk-ins are not accepted.

What I do.

Blackwork

Bold, high-contrast designs with saturated black ink. From solid fills to intricate patterns, blackwork has an impact that ages beautifully.

Tribal · Geometric fills · Abstract · Negative space

FAQ

Common questions.

How much does a tattoo cost?

Pricing depends on size, complexity, and placement. Small pieces start at $200. Larger custom work is quoted per project after your consultation. A deposit is required to hold your booking.

Do you do walk-ins?

No. Axiom is a private studio that operates by appointment only. All bookings start with a consultation.

How far in advance do I need to book?

Current wait time is approximately 6–8 weeks. Availability varies — submit a booking request and Kai will confirm the earliest available slot.

What should I do to prepare for my appointment?

Stay hydrated, eat a full meal beforehand, wear comfortable clothing that gives easy access to the placement area, and avoid alcohol for 24 hours before your session.

Are touch-ups included?

Yes. Free touch-ups are included within 6 months of your original appointment, provided aftercare instructions were followed.

Do you tattoo cover-ups?

Cover-up work is taken on selectively and requires an in-person consultation or high-resolution photos of the existing tattoo. Submit via the booking form and Kai will advise on feasibility.

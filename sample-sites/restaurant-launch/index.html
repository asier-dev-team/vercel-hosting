Our Philosophy

Guided by what the season brings to the table.

At Solstice Table, we believe the finest dining begins long before the first course is plated — it begins at dawn in our partner farms, in the quiet decisions of what to harvest and when.

Our menu changes with the solstices and equinoxes, four times a year, honoring the rhythms of the land and delivering an experience that can never quite be repeated.

Chef Amélie Fontaine spent fifteen years in Lyon and Kyoto before returning to New York to open a restaurant that reflects both the discipline of classical French technique and the reverence for seasonal purity she found in Japan.
